https://expo.dev/accounts/lebardy/projects/final-project/builds/b2f3d5c6-1abb-486d-bca3-57779ac62d2a

### Android

1. Download the APK file from the build link provided
2. On your Android device, enable "Install from Unknown Sources" in Settings
3. Open the downloaded APK file
4. Tap "Install" when prompted
5. Open the app from your app drawer

## Getting Started

### Sign Up

1. Open the app
2. Enter your email address
3. Create a password
4. Tap "Create Account"

### Sign In

1. Enter your email and password
2. Tap "Sign in"

## Using the App

### View the Feed

- The main screen shows all posts from all users
- Posts are sorted by newest first
- Pull down to refresh and see new posts
- Tap on images/videos to view them in full screen

### Create a Post

1. Tap the **+** button in the bottom-right corner
2. Type your message (optional)
3. Tap "Pick Image or Video" to add media (optional)
4. Select a photo or video from your gallery
5. Tap "Post" to share

### Edit Your Posts

1. Find one of your posts in the feed
2. Tap the **Edit** button
3. Modify your text
4. Tap "Save"

### Delete Your Posts

1. Find one of your posts in the feed
2. Tap the **Delete** button
3. Confirm deletion

### Sign Out

- Tap "Sign out" in the top-right corner of the feed screen

## Features

- **Share text posts** - Express yourself with words
- **Share photos and videos** - Upload images or videos from your gallery
- **Public feed** - See posts from all users in one place
- **Edit your posts** - Make changes to your content anytime
- **Delete your posts** - Remove posts you no longer want
- **Secure authentication** - Your account is protected with email/password

## Tips

- You can create posts with just text, just media, or both
- Only you can edit or delete your own posts
- The feed updates automatically when you pull down to refresh
- Make sure you have an internet connection to view and create posts
